# ShopKeeper - Summary

## What Was Built

A web app disguised as a cozy bakery game that secretly teaches business skills. Grandparent-friendly design (big fonts, warm colors, simple interactions) with real B2B mechanics underneath: invoicing, profit margins, inventory management, cash flow, waste reduction, customer relations, and pricing strategy. 7 skills are "discovered" through play.

## Tech Decisions

- **Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS** — zero dependencies, opens instantly
- **Grandparent-friendly UI** — 18px base font, warm bakery palette, big tap targets
- **Separate engine from UI** — engine.js is fully testable with Node.js
- **Seeded random** — deterministic customer generation for reproducible gameplay
- **localStorage** — persistent state across sessions

## Potential Next Steps

- Seasonal events (holiday specials, sales)
- Supplier negotiation mini-game
- Tax calculation feature (secretly teaching accounting)
- Multi-language support for non-English-speaking grandparents

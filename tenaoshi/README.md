# 手直し (Tenaoshi) - ご近所リフォームゲーム

A cozy top-down neighborhood renovation game built with Raylib (C).

## Concept

You are a recently retired craftsperson with decades of DIY skills. Your quiet neighborhood has seen better days — fences are broken, gardens are overgrown, walls need painting. Walk around the tile-based town map, discover neighbors' requests, and fix things up one house at a time.

As you complete repairs, the neighborhood visually transforms from run-down to beautiful. A relaxing, satisfying experience designed for seniors who love DIY.

## Features

- **タイルマップ探索**: Walk freely around a charming neighborhood map
- **修理タスク**: Find and fix various problems (broken fences, peeling paint, overgrown gardens, leaky roofs)
- **ビフォー・アフター**: Watch buildings transform as you complete repairs
- **道具選択**: Choose the right tool for each job (hammer, paintbrush, shears, wrench)
- **ご近所交流**: Neighbors thank you and the town's happiness score rises
- **のんびりペース**: No time pressure, no enemies — just peaceful craftsmanship

## Tech Stack

- **Language**: C11
- **Graphics**: Raylib 5.5
- **Build**: Makefile (macOS/Linux)
- **No external dependencies** beyond Raylib

## How to Run

```bash
make
./tenaoshi
```

## Controls

- **矢印キー / WASD**: 移動
- **スペース**: 調べる / 修理する
- **1-4**: 道具を選択
- **ESC**: メニュー

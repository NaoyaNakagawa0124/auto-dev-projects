# SproutMap — Build Summary

## What Was Built

A p5.js generative art app where meals become a living garden. 36 foods across 7 categories, each mapped to a unique plant type. Vegetables grow into leafy greens, fruits become flowers, grains become wheat stalks, proteins become mushrooms. Sustainability scoring grades your diet A-F. Save your garden as a PNG.

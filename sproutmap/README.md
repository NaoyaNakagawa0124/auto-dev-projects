# SproutMap 🌱

> Your meals become a living garden. Eat well, grow beauty.

## What is SproutMap?

A p5.js generative art app where every meal you log grows into a plant in your personal garden. Vegetables become leafy greens, grains become wheat stalks, fruits become flowers, proteins become mushrooms. Over time, your food choices paint a unique, living landscape.

The greener and more diverse your diet, the more lush your garden. It's a beautiful way to see your food sustainability footprint.

## Features

1. **Meal Logger** — Add what you ate with simple category buttons
2. **Living Garden** — p5.js procedural plants grow based on your food history
3. **Sustainability Score** — Color-coded footprint (green = sustainable, amber = moderate, red = high impact)
4. **Sharable Art** — Save your garden as a PNG screenshot

## Try It

```bash
cd sproutmap && open index.html
```

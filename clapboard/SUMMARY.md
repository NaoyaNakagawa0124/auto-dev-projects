# ClapBoard - Summary

## What Was Built

A Chrome extension that gamifies work time tracking with movie production metaphors. Inspired by the 🎬 clapperboard emoji. "Action!" starts timing, "Cut!" stops, "Retake" restarts, "That's a Wrap!" ends the day. Productions (projects), scenes (tasks), takes (retries), dailies (daily review), and a director rank system.

## Potential Next Steps

- Export time logs to CSV for invoicing
- Keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Shift+A for Action, Ctrl+Shift+C for Cut)
- Background timer that persists when popup closes
- Team mode where colleagues are your "cast"

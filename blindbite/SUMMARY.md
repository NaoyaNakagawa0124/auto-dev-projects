# BlindBite - Summary

## What Was Built

The anti-DoorDash Chrome extension. Instead of overwhelming you with choices, it gives you ONE mystery restaurant. 12 cuisines, 60 restaurants, 15 supply chain facts, trust scoring with 5 levels, streak tracking. Addresses the real problem of decision fatigue (86% of Gen Z have menu anxiety).

## Potential Next Steps

- Real restaurant data via Google Places API
- Location-based picks using browser geolocation
- "Food passport" tracking which cuisines you've explored
- Social sharing of mystery picks

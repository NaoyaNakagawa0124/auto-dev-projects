# CommuteStory - Summary

## What Was Built

A Jupyter notebook narrating a daily train commute through 8 stations of infrastructure engineering. Each station is a chapter: steel-truss terminal, granite tunnel (TBM-bored), cable-stayed bridge, AI smart station, BRT interchange, concrete viaduct, living green station, and multi-modal hub. 7 Plotly visualizations with real engineering data.

## Potential Next Steps

- Interactive station selection (click map to read chapter)
- Real transit data integration (GTFS feeds)
- Audio narration for actual commuters
- City-specific editions (NYC, London, Tokyo)

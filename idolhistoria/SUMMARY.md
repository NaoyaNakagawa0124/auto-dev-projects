# Idol Historia - Summary

## What Was Built

**アイドルヒストリア (Idol Historia)** is a rich Jupyter notebook that visualizes 50+ years of Japanese idol culture history. From the birth of the "idol" concept in 1971 through the AKB48 revolution to VTubers — 31 major milestones across 7 eras, with 5 interactive Plotly charts. Includes a special "March 31" section exploring how this date connects to idol graduation culture.

## Key Features

- **Interactive milestone timeline**: 31 events from 1971-2025 with era color coding
- **Era radar charts**: Compare 7 eras across 5 dimensions (debuts, group size, fan engagement, tech, global reach)
- **Debut pattern analysis**: Stacked bar chart of solo vs group vs virtual debuts by decade
- **Genre evolution stream**: How idol music shifted from kayokyoku to EDM/metal over 50 years
- **"March 31" special**: What happened on this day in idol history (graduation culture)
- **Fan culture metrics**: 4-panel showing distance, participation, spending, and diversity evolution
- **Rich narrative**: Japanese markdown commentary throughout

## Tech Stack

- Python 3 / Jupyter Notebook
- Plotly (5 interactive charts: scatter, radar, bar, area, multi-panel)
- Pandas / NumPy (data processing)
- Matplotlib (font config)

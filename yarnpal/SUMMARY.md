# YarnPal — Build Summary

## What Was Built

A grandparent-friendly knitting/crochet PWA inspired by 🧶. Three screens with huge buttons, large text, and warm colors. Row/stitch counter with undo, project list with yarn colors and needle sizes, and a session timer. Installable on phones, works offline.

## Design Principles

- **BIG everything** — 96px counter numbers, 80px tap targets, 20px+ font sizes
- **Warm, cozy palette** — Cream background, crimson accents, soft shadows
- **No confusion** — Three clear tabs, no nested menus, no settings buried
- **Forgiving** — Undo button always available, confirm before reset

# FoodTrend - Summary

## What Was Built

A Jupyter notebook with 7 Plotly visualizations for aspiring food TikTokers. Engagement heatmap, trending categories, hashtag analysis, competitor benchmarks, content gap finder, growth projection, and strategy summary. One-hand friendly — just Shift+Enter.
